About the project

Simple, honest and practical.

My name is Mariusz Sobiesinski. I am a radio communication enthusiast from Germany. I created PoC Radio Lab Europe to test PoC radios during normal daily use and to provide manufacturers with clear, honest and useful feedback.

This is an independent personal project, not a large company or laboratory. The focus is on repeatable field observations rather than marketing claims.

Testing environments

Four practical settings.

🚛

Road transport

Daily truck use, LTE continuity, audio clarity, ergonomics and battery behaviour.

🏭

Industrial workplace

Where available: indoor halls, metal structures, walls, noise and practical communication.

🏠

Home use

Setup, charging, ease of use, Wi-Fi operation and everyday communication.

🌳

Outdoor use

Portability, network stability, display visibility and voice quality outside.

Standard evaluation process

  1. Unboxing and visual inspection
  2. Initial setup and configuration
  3. Battery, audio and network observations
  4. Real-world field use
  5. Strengths, weaknesses and practical recommendations
  6. Final written report with photographs
